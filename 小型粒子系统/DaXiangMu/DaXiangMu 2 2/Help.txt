Keypad instructions
* the digital key 1,3 control Angle moves up and down on the y axis.
* the number key 2,4,6,8 control up and down the left and right translation.
* digital key 5 reset perspective.
* numeric keys 7,9 control particle size.
* +, - zoom in Angle.
* the direction key up and down, the left and right control the particle's y axis and the x axis jet strength.
* w increases gravity, and s reduces gravity.
* a slows down particle velocity, and d increases particle velocity.
* p suspended
* c play
Attention! 
Because the color mixture is enabled and the texture of the load has its own color.
So the final effect of the color setting will be different.
